About TBS

The Branch Sprinters (TBS) is a competitive Gorilla Tag clan built around speed, skill, and teamwork. Our mission is to push the limits of Gorilla Tag movement and build one of the most respected communities in the game.

Features:
  • Almost every Weekend: Training and Scrims
  • Competitive events and tournaments
  • Friendly community and support for new members

THE BRANCH SPRINTER RULES®

1. Respect Everyone

No harassment, bullying, racism, sexism, or hate speech.

No toxic behavior in-game or voice chat.

2. Follow Gorilla Tag Rules

No cheating, modding in public lobbies or exploiting glitches.

3. Discord Behavior

No spam, excessive caps or mic spamming.

No NSFW content.

4. Competitive Play

Play fair during scrims and tournaments.

No stream sniping.

5. Staff Authority

Respect moderators and clan leaders.

6. Voice Chat Rules

No screaming or earrape.

7. Content & Streaming

Represent the clan positively in videos and streams.

8. Consequences

Warning • Temporary mute • Kick • Permanent ban

9. Have Fun

Enjoy the game and improve together.

10. Sick Leave

Create a ticket if you cannot play a scrim.

11 Other Servers/Teams

Being in another clan can lead to removal.
