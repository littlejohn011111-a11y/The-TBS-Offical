Our clan trains together, improves together, and builds one of the strongest Gorilla Tag communities.

The Branch Sprinters Rules®

1. Respect Everyone

No harassment, bullying, racism, sexism, or hate speech.

No toxic behavior in-game or in voice chat.

Treat all members with respect, regardless of skill level.

2. Follow Gorilla Tag Rules

Follow the official rules of Gorilla Tag.

No cheating, modding in public lobbies, or exploiting glitches.

No ban evasion.

3. Discord Behavior

Keep chats in the correct channels.

No spam, excessive caps, or mic spamming.

No NSFW content.

4. Competitive Play

Play fair during scrims and tournaments.

No stream sniping.

Listen to team leaders during matches.

Practice good sportsmanship | win or lose.

Play the Scrim, when you didn't create a Ticket in Sick Leave!

5. Staff Authority

Respect moderators and clan leaders.

If a staff member gives a warning, take it seriously.

Do not argue publicly — use tickets or DMs for issues.

6. Voice Chat Rules

No screaming or earrape.

Use push-to-talk if you have background noise.

Don’t talk over others constantly.

7. Content & Streaming

Ask permission before recording private scrims.

Represent the clan positively in videos and streams.

8. Consequences

Breaking rules may result in:

  • Warning
  • Temporary mute
  • Kick from clan
  • Permanent ban

9. Have Fun!

We’re here to enjoy the game, improve together, and build friendships. Keep it positive and competitive in a healthy way.

10. Sick Leave

If you are not able to play a Scrim, please create a Ticket in Sick Leave.

11. Other Servers/Teams

If you're on another Discord server or in another team, that counts as betraying our clan. If you're in another clan and we find out, you'll be kicked from the team.
