About The Clan

The Branch Sprinters (TBS) is a competitive Gorilla Tag clan built for players who love speed, skill and teamwork. Inside Gorilla Tag, movement is everything. From insane wall climbs to fast branch jukes and intense chases through the forest, the best players dominate with movement and strategy. TBS trains to master these mechanics and push our members to the next level. Every weekend we host training sessions where members practice movement, routes, tagging strategies and teamwork. But TBS is more than just skill — it's a community where players can meet new friends, have fun and grow together. If you love Gorilla Tag and want to become faster, smarter and stronger, then The Branch Sprinters is the place for you. Join the team and become part of the sprint.
