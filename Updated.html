About The Branch Sprinters

The Branch Sprinters (TBS) is a competitive Gorilla Tag clan focused on speed, movement mastery and teamwork. Inside Gorilla Tag, the best players dominate through advanced movement, insane wall climbs and smart jukes through the forest. Our clan trains every weekend to push our members to the next level while building a strong community.

THE BRANCH SPRINTER RULES®

1. Respect Everyone

No harassment, bullying, racism, sexism, or hate speech. Treat all members with respect.

2. Follow Gorilla Tag Rules

Follow official rules. No cheating, modding in public lobbies or exploiting glitches.

3. Discord Behavior

Use correct channels, no spam or NSFW content.

4. Competitive Play

Play fair during scrims and tournaments. Practice good sportsmanship.

5. Staff Authority

Respect moderators and clan leaders.

6. Voice Chat Rules

No screaming or mic spamming.

7. Content & Streaming

Ask permission before recording private scrims.

8. Consequences

Breaking rules may result in warnings, mutes, kicks or bans.

9. Have Fun

Enjoy the game, improve together and build friendships.

10. Sick Leave

If you cannot play a scrim, create a ticket in Discord.

11. Other Servers/Teams

Being in another clan counts as betrayal and may result in removal.
